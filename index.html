Spy x Family

Spy x Family is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Tatsuya Endo. The story follows a master spy, known as Twilight, who is assigned a top-secret mission that requires him to create a fake family to infiltrate an elite school. To maintain his cover, he adopts a young girl named Anya, who secretly possesses telepathic abilities, and enters a fake marriage with Yor, an assassin who hides her true identity. Unaware of each other's secrets, the three form an unconventional yet heartwarming family. The series masterfully blends action, comedy, and emotional moments, as the characters struggle to maintain their double lives while growing closer as a real family. With clever writing, engaging characters, and a unique premise, Spy x Family has become a hit among fans worldwide. Its themes of trust, love, and the meaning of family resonate deeply, making it both entertaining and touching.

GenreAction, Comedy, Detective
Directed byKazuhiro Furuhashi, Takahiro Harada
Episodes37
First episode dateApril 9, 2022

The Apothecary Diaries

The Apothecary Diaries is a Japanese historical mystery series set in an imperial court inspired by ancient China. It follows Maomao, a witty and sharp-tongued young woman with a background in herbal medicine and toxicology. After being kidnapped and sold into servitude at the palace, she conceals her intelligence to avoid attention. However, her keen insight into medical cases and poisons soon catches the eye of Jinshi, a high-ranking and enigmatic court official. As Maomao solves unusual illnesses and courtly intrigues, she becomes entangled in the complex dynamics of imperial politics and hidden secrets. The story blends suspense, humor, and romance, while showcasing Maomao's unique perspective and scientific knowledge in an era dominated by tradition and hierarchy. Rich in historical detail and layered characters, The Apothecary Diaries offers a refreshing and intelligent narrative that stands out among period dramas and mystery series alike.

GenreRomance, Drama, Mystery
Directed byNorihiro Naganuma, Akinori Fudesaka
Episodes42
First episode dateOctober 22, 2023

Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime and manga series that blends supernatural action with dark fantasy. The story centers on Yuji Itadori, a high school student with extraordinary physical strength who becomes involved in the world of Jujutsu Sorcerers after swallowing a cursed object—one of the fingers of Ryomen Sukuna, a powerful and malevolent Curse. As a result, Yuji becomes the host of Sukuna, gaining access to immense power while risking his own humanity. Under the guidance of his eccentric teacher Satoru Gojo, Yuji joins Tokyo Jujutsu High, where he trains to fight deadly Curses and protect humanity. The series explores themes of life, death, and the value of human emotion through intense battles, strategic combat, and complex character development. Known for its stunning animation, emotional storytelling, and rich world-building, Jujutsu Kaisen has quickly become a standout title in modern shonen anime and manga.

GenreAdventure, Dark fantasy, Supernatural
Directed bySunghoo Park, Shōta Goshozono
Episodes47
First episode dateOctober 3, 2020
